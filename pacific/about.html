Welcome to Pacific Trails Resort

Discover tranquility and peace at Pacific Trails Resort, nestled in the heart of nature's paradise. Our resort offers a unique blend of comfort, adventure, and harmony with the environment.

Our Story

Founded in 1968, Pacific Trails Resort started as a vision to create a serene escape in the midst of nature's beauty. Our journey from a small retreat to a premier destination reflects our commitment to preserving the natural environment while providing exceptional guest experiences.

Meet the Team

Our dedicated team, led by Isabella Arenas, is passionate about creating memorable stays for our guests. Each member brings their love for nature and hospitality to ensure your time with us is unforgettable.

Our Commitment

We are committed to sustainability, guest satisfaction, and community involvement. From eco-friendly practices to personalized guest services, we strive to make a positive impact on both our environment and the community.
